I. The Declaration of Data Sovereignty

The current paradigm of medical AI is broken. Global oncology breakthroughs are held hostage by data silos, privacy theater, and the exorbitant costs of centralized compute.

We believe that the cure for cancer should not be a trade secret of a single corporation, nor should it require the sacrifice of patient privacy.

The Sovereign Mohawk Ecosystem exists to build a planetary-scale, formally verified intelligence layer where data remains local, but insights become global.

II. The Technical Covenant

We commit to a standard of Privacy by Mathematical Proof, not just Privacy by Promise. Every node in the Mohawk network adheres to:

  • Formal Verification: Sovereign-Mohawk-Proto is framed for formally checked aggregation controls in the live demo.
  • Active Privacy Budgeting: Clinical-grade privacy budget with epsilon approximately 0.61.
  • Hardware Attestation: TPM quote acceptance to verify secure, untampered compute.

III. The Sustainability Mandate

Centralized AI is a resource parasite. Mohawk is built for the Green AI era. By shifting intelligence to the edge, we target:

  • 75% energy reduction through distributed local training.
  • Zero-water cooling through standard edge infrastructure.
  • E-waste mitigation with a 5-7 year Sovereign Node lifespan.

IV. The MOHWK Economic Engine

We reject speculative pump-and-dump cycles. The MOHWK token is designed as a Burn-and-Mint Equilibrium model:

  • Work: Nodes provide verified oncology research to earn rewards.
  • Usage: Institutions burn MOHWK to access collective intelligence across the global map.
  • Governance: Token holders vote on protocol upgrades and DPIA compliance standards.
